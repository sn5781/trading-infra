Dietary gates
01
≥10g long-chain fat per meal
Gate: ≥10g at each meal — no MCT/coconut oil
EVERY MEAL
▸ examples
Count the fat grams, not volume. Sources that work:
~15g macadamia nuts = 10g fat ✓
~21g peanuts = 10g fat ✓
1 tbsp olive oil = 14g fat ✓
¼ avocado = 8g fat ✓
MCT oil / coconut milk = does NOT count
This is the highest-priority gate. Gallbladder cannot empty below ~10g LCT per meal.
02
Fiber 25–30g/day, no inulin/wheat bran
Gate: <35g total daily fiber, soluble sources only
DAILY TOTAL
▸ examples
Reduce 5g every 3–5 days from previous 50g+ intake. Track daily total.
Avoid: inulin, FOS, wheat bran, chicory root
Prefer: psyllium husk, oats, white rice, banana
App option: Cronometer or MyFitnessPal fiber column
03
Fructose <15g per sitting, no excess-fructose fruit
Gate: no apples / pears / mango / dried fruit / honey today
PER MEAL
▸ safe/unsafe list
AVOID: apple, pear, mango, watermelon, cherries, dried fruit, grapes, agave, HFCS, fruit juice

OK: banana, blueberry, strawberry, orange, kiwi, raspberry, cantaloupe
80% of adults fail a 50g fructose breath test. Threshold for symptoms is ~25g/sitting.
04
A2 dairy or no dairy
Gate: zero A1 β-casein dairy — no standard lactose-free milk/yogurt
ALL DAY
▸ note
Lactose-free milk still contains A1 β-casein. A1 releases BCM-7, an opioid peptide that delays GI transit and increases inflammation. Run a 2–4 week elimination. A2 milk or no dairy = pass.
Movement gates
05
Calisthenics only ≥2h after a fat-containing meal
Gate: never train fasted — pre-meal must include fat + carbs
BEFORE TRAINING
▸ pre-workout meal
Low-fiber, moderate carb, low-fat (≤15g), eaten 2h before. Fat present = gallbladder empties before IAP spikes.
Option A: Low-fat yogurt 150g + 15g macadamia nuts + banana
Option B: White rice + small portion fish or egg
Option C: Rice cakes + thin nut butter (2 tsp) + banana
Splanchnic blood flow drops 80% within 10 min of intense exercise. A fed gut tolerates this far better.
06
≥60 min low-intensity walking/day
Gate: ≥1 post-meal walk ≥15 min at easy pace
POST-MEAL
▸ why
Postprandial walking outperformed prokinetic medication in an RCT (Hosseini-Asl, 2021). Accelerates gastric emptying 13%. Target: 3× 15–20 min walks after each meal. Outdoor/hiking sessions count double.
Minimum effective dose: 10–15 min after eating
Target: 60–120 min total across day
Meal structure
07
At least 2 meals today (no OMAD)
Gate: ≥2 meals spaced ≥4h apart, each with ≥10g LCT fat
MEAL TIMING
▸ transition pace
Phase 1 (week 1–2): 2 meals, 6–8h apart → Phase 2 (week 2+): 3 meals, 4–5h apart. Each gap allows 1 MMC cycle (90–230 min fasting motility sweep). Splitting same calories across meals: reduces peak fermentation, restores 6–8 gallbladder empties/day vs 2–3 on OMAD.
